Password Day

Today it's world password day.
You know passwords are the first step to secure your account. If you choose a good password, you can make your account to be safe.
Choosing a good password is important, and to this day we have a few steps for you to choose a better password.
When you're choosing your password, it should be atleast 8+ characters long and contain special characters with numbers.
The most frequent password is the word "password".
In your passwords, it's not recommended to use your name, email, business name, favorite stuff or any other similiar stuff.
You password can be something nobody knows you will ever guess.
For a business account password, make sure to don't use the name of your business, its @, email, address and stuff. This "rule" can be also used for personal account.

Just, make sure to choose the strongest password you can. You can also use services to save your passwords, such as 1password.com or check out if your data are in danger.
Hope this article was helpful.
